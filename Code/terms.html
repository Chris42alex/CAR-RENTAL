    ELIGIBILITY AND REQUIREMENTS

     

     AGE RESTRICTIONS

     When renting vehicles from Voitore, the following age limits are observed: 

    Minimum 21 years old
    Maximum 70 years old

     

    CONCURRENT RENTALS

    The hirer is not allowed to rent more than 1 vehicle at the same time. If the hirer has an existing active rental that coincides with a new booking’s scheduled pick-up, Voitore may have the right to refuse the new booking.

     

    REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

    The hirer is required to provide the following original, valid documents when renting a vehicle from Voitore. All documents have to be under the hirer's name:

    UAE Residents:

            - UAE Driving License

            - Emirates ID

            - Credit card* (valid for the next 3 months minimum)

            - Passport with Residence visa

     

    Non-UAE Residents:

            - International Driving Permit / GCC driving license / valid driving license from their country of origin (refer to Driving License Exemptions list)

            - Passport with entry stamp

            - Credit card* (valid for the next 3 months minimum)

     

    * The same credit card is not allowed to be used on 2 or more concurrent rentals.

    A rental with less than 24-hour duration will be considered as a 1-day rental. Similarly, any excess rental period that is less than 24 hours is considered as an additional 1-day rental.

    NO SHOW

    AED200 fee will be charged if the customer fails to show up for a prepaid booking that has not been cancelled prior to the pick-up schedule.

     

    LATE ARRIVAL

    If a client makes a prepaid booking but fails to arrive at the specified pick-up location within 2 working hours of the pick-up schedule (or if the location closes before then, by the location’s closing time), the vehicle may still be provided subject to availability. The pick-up and drop-off timings per the voucher will be adhered to.

     

    RENTAL EXTENSION

    For rentals booked in the Voitore UAE website, the hirer may extend their active rental by creating a new booking with pick-up date and time matching their current rental’s off-hire date and time. The website rates at the time of new booking shall apply on the rental extension.

    If the hirer fails to do so, they may request for the rental extension at any Voitore rental desk, and the local rates and policies will apply on the rental extension.

     

    EARLY RETURN

    Daily & Weekly Rental: There will be no refund for unused car rental days once a Daily or Weekly rental has started.

     

    Monthly Rental: Fixed monthly rate will apply for hire periods between 21-30 days; pro-rata is not applicable. If a hirer returns the car within less than 21 days, doing so will automatically cancel the monthly plan, and the corresponding daily or weekly rate will apply.

      

    LATE RETURN

    Only 1-hour grace period for late return is allowed. Beyond this, rental charges will apply per local rates and policies.

     

    PAYMENT AND FEES

     

    VALUE-ADDED TAX (VAT)

    Effective January 2018, Voitore Car Rental reserves the right to add value-added tax (VAT) on all services and rates, as confirmed and implemented by the UAE Government. This is applicable on all bookings for pick-up from 01-Jan-2018.

     

    RATES VALIDITY

    Voitore Car Rental reserves the right to change the prices of its products and services.

     

    PAYMENT

    All prices are payable in UAE Dirhams (AED). Cash and all major credit cards are accepted as means of payment. Advance payment for the whole rental is required prior to collecting the vehicle from the counter.For bookings made on the Voitore UAE website, the credit card used online must be presented at the time of pick up.

     

    SECURITY DEPOSIT

    At the time of vehicle pick-up, the main driver shall leave a security deposit, which shall be blocked on their credit card. Cash and debit cards are not accepted as security deposit. The rental agent shall confirm the amount of security block at the time of vehicle pick-up.

    If the security deposit is not provided, Voitore Car Rental reserves the right to refuse the rental.

    INSURANCE AND PROTECTION

     

    PERSONAL ACCIDENT INSURANCE

    The hirer may purchase the Personal Accident Insurance (PAI) for added security for the vehicle occupants. This provides for compensation in the unfortunate event of an accident involving the rented vehicle that results to physical injury or death of the occupant(s). Note that PAI does not cover hospitalizations.

     

    ADDITIONAL INSURANCE /

    PROTECTION OPTIONS

    The hirer may purchase additional vehicle insurance for their rental to reduce their financial liability for damage to the Voitore vehicle, its parts and accessories, provided the vehicle is used in accordance with the terms and conditions of the hire agreement.

            - Collision Damage Waiver (CDW)

            - Super Collision Damage Waiver (SCDW)

     

     

    INSURANCE EXCESS 

    In the event of an accident involving the rented vehicle that results to vehicle damage, the payable insurance excess is based on the result of the police report and type of additional vehicle insurance availed by the hirer at the time of booking or hiring the vehicle.

            - Personal belongings or luggage are not insured.

            - Voitore is not responsible for any personal belongings/luggage left inside the vehicle upon off-hire.

A pre-authorisation will be taken on the credit card to cover excess & other additional charges at the time of vehicle hire. Standard Voitore UAE Terms and Conditions apply. All rentals may be subject to additional surcharges (e.g. for special equipment, additional drivers, young drivers, optional insurances, fuel, Salik, Traffic fine etc.).
    All rentals are subject to an insurance excess, optional insurances are available to excess.

    Voitore Car Rental UAE applies Cancellation and No Show/Lost Rental charges on all prepaid rates booked from www.Voitoreuae.com

    3. Refunds will be made to the credit card that the original booking was made on within 21 working days.
    4. In the event of cancellation of a pre-paid booking due to non conformance or availability of the documents with the customer, an Admin fee of AED 50.00 will be charged to the customer.

    Late Arrival Policy:

    If you make a reservation specifying a pick-up location and you do not arrive at the specified pick-up location for the rental within 4 hours of the specified pick-up time, (or if the location closes before then, by the locations closing time), the reservation will be treated as a No Show and charged accordingly.

    Your extras, i.e. GPS, Baby seat etc. are payable at the rental location & is subject to availability.

    Front side copy of credit card along with NOC for credit card charges of the hirer of the vehicle should have a mandatory minimum validity of 3 months. If this is not available then Voitore reserves the right to refuse the rental. Preauthorization on the credit card to cover the estimated cost of the rental will be taken.
